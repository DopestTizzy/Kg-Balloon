About

Welcome to kg balloon!

Kg Balloon was established in 2019. Then the idea struck during the Covid-19 pandemic. We knew we had to do something since we were under lockdown, starting a balloon business that creates and delivers any kind of balloons to customers was the answer and so the journey of kg balloon began.

Kg balloon offers so many options and designs such as balloon garlands, balloon bouquets, Hot-Air balloon, and we even go further and beyond by creating Halo Crowns for all age groups and for any occasion. Kg balloon caters for occasions such as baby showers, birthday parties, graduations, engagement parties, car dealerships, and shopping malls.

Mission

To stimulate a perfect sense of occasion and to provide a memorable experience to our customers and to become the leader in the balloon event by offering the best services at competitive prices across all sides and we offer an enjoyable customer service experience and deliver superior quality.

Vision

To fill the world with happiness and laughter through
the joy and the art of balloons.
